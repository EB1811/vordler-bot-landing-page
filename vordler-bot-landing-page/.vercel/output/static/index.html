VordlerBot lets ChatGPT decide if Twitch messages are appropriate or not, and delete them for you.

How It Works

1. Sign In

Sign in using Twitch. Your account will be automatically tied to the account you log in with.

2. Join Channel

Click the "Join Channel" button in the dashboard and follow the instructions to mod VordlerBot in your channel.

3. Customize

Customize your moderation settings, including the GPT prompt, enable our GPT commands, and add your own commands with custom prompts.
